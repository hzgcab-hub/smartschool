Student Management

Complete student lifecycle management from enrollment to graduation with detailed profiles and progress tracking. Like Eldana's journey through O-Class.

Teacher Portal

Empower teachers with tools for attendance, grading, and CCA (Continuous Classroom Assessment) methodology. Support 102k+ trained educators.

Finance (ETB)

Complete financial management with Ethiopian Birr support, school grants tracking, and expense reports aligned with GEQIP-E initiatives.

Exam & Results

Create exams, enter marks, generate report cards, and analyze student performance with beautiful charts. Track transition rates to Grade 2.

Inclusive Education

Support for students with special needs through 1,400+ Inclusive Education Resource Centers. Track resources like wheelchairs, styluses, and voice recorders.

Refugee Support

Manage 52 refugee schools, 1,346 incentive teachers, and track educational resources for displaced communities across Ethiopia.
